About Pack 776

Our Mission

We follow the Cub Scout Promise and Law of the Pack. We build character, develop leadership, and create lifelong memories through exciting activities.

Activities

Pinewood Derby, camping trips, hikes, service projects, STEM activities, and more! We meet monthly and have den meetings.

Who Can Join?

Boys and girls in Kindergarten through 5th grade living in Conroe and surrounding areas.

Why Join Cub Scouts?

Adventure & Fun

Outdoor activities, games, and exciting field trips.

Friendship & Teamwork

Build strong friendships and learn to work as a team.

Character Development

Learn values that last a lifetime.
