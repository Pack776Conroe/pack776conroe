Join Cub Scout Pack 776

We're excited to have you join our pack! Cub Scouts is open to boys and girls in kindergarten through 5th grade.

Registration Steps

  1. Contact our Pack leadership using the form below or call/text the number on the Contact page.
  2. Complete the online registration through the official BSA website (my.scouting.org).
  3. Attend a pack meeting to meet the families!

Annual Dues: Approximately $150-$200 (includes national registration, pack fees, and handbook). Scholarships available.
